Drivers - City Type


The pie chart displayed above showcases the percentage of drivers that operate in each city type.


The color of each individual segment represents the city type:

  • Urban - Coral
  • Suburban - Light Blue
  • Rural - Gold


Below are some trends that were observed based on the data in the plot:

  • There are 4x more drivers in Urban cities than both Suburban and Urban combined
  • There are 8x more drivers in Suburban cities than in Rural cities
